# Nimbus Robotics IT Security Policy

<!--
Teaching note:
Security policies are a common RAG use case because they contain private rules
that are different for every organization.
-->

## Account Security

Every Nimbus Robotics employee must use multi-factor authentication for company
accounts. The preferred MFA method is an authenticator app, not SMS.

## Password Rules

Passwords must be at least **14 characters** long. Passwords must not reuse the
employee's name, the word Nimbus, or a robot product name.

## Device Locking

Company laptops must lock automatically after **7 minutes** of inactivity. This
rule applies in the office, at home, and while traveling.

## Data Classification

Nimbus Robotics uses three fictional data labels:

- Public: approved marketing material.
- Internal: normal company documents and routine team notes.
- Fogbound: sensitive private data, including unreleased robot designs and
  customer warehouse maps.

## Sharing Fogbound Data

Fogbound data may only be shared with approved employees through the company
document system. It must not be pasted into public chat tools or personal email.

## USB Storage

USB storage devices are disabled by default. A temporary exception can be
requested from IT for hardware lab testing, and the exception expires after
**48 hours**.

## Incident Reporting

Lost devices, suspicious logins, and accidental sharing of Fogbound data must be
reported within **30 minutes** in the fictional channel `#security-watch`.

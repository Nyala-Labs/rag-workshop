## What is Skylark 3?

**Skylark 3** is Nimbus Robotics' fictional compact warehouse robot. It moves
small bins between storage shelves and packing stations.

## Payload

Skylark 3 can carry a maximum payload of **22 kilograms**. The robot refuses a
route if its onboard scale detects a bin heavier than that limit.

## Battery Life

A fully charged Skylark 3 battery lasts **9.5 hours** during normal warehouse
operation. Heavy traffic, frequent lifting, and cold loading docks can reduce
that time.

## Charging

Skylark 3 uses the **HaloDock Mini** charger. A normal charge from 20 percent to
90 percent takes about **46 minutes**.

## Safety Sensors

The robot uses three fictional sensor systems:

- LumaRing: a front light curtain for nearby motion.
- PebbleSense: a floor texture sensor that detects loose objects.
- QuietStop: a low-noise emergency brake system.

## Connectivity

Skylark 3 connects to warehouse Wi-Fi using WPA3. If Wi-Fi drops, the robot
keeps its current position and waits for the Cloud Fleet controller to reconnect.

## Maintenance

The blue wheel cartridges should be inspected every **120 operating hours**.
The dust filter should be replaced every **six weeks** in dusty facilities.

## Warranty

Nimbus Robotics provides a **two-year fictional warranty** for Skylark 3 units.
The warranty does not cover damage caused by carrying more than 22 kilograms.

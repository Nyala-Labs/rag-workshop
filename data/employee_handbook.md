## Company Overview

Nimbus Robotics builds small warehouse robots for indoor logistics teams. The
company was founded in 2021 and its fictional headquarters are in Port Azure,
Oregon.

Nimbus Robotics has three internal teams:

- Cloud Fleet: the software team that manages robot routing.
- Hardware Lab: the team that designs sensors and charging docks.
- Customer Success: the team that trains warehouse operators.

## Work Hours

Nimbus Robotics uses a flexible schedule called **Core Cloud Hours**. Employees
may start any time between 7:00 AM and 10:00 AM local time, but everyone is
expected to be available from **10:00 AM to 2:00 PM local time** for meetings and
collaboration.

## Remote Work

Employees may work remotely up to **three days per week**. New employees spend
their first two Tuesdays onsite for lab safety orientation and team setup.

## Vacation Policy

Full-time employees receive **18 Nimbus Days** of paid vacation each calendar
year. Unused Nimbus Days do not roll over, because the company wants employees
to actually take rest time.

## Learning Stipend

Each employee has a yearly learning stipend of **$900**. The stipend can be used
for courses, technical books, conferences, or certification exams related to the
employee's role.

## Lunch Benefit

On Wednesdays, Nimbus Robotics provides a team lunch called **Cloud Table**.
The default vegetarian meal is the **Cumulus Bowl**, which includes rice,
roasted vegetables, chickpeas, and lemon tahini sauce.

## Internal Help

Employees can ask handbook questions in the fictional Slack channel
`#ask-people-ops`. Urgent payroll questions should be sent to
`payroll@nimbus-robotics.example`.
